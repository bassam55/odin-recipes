Whipped Coffee Iced Latte

Description

For a quick iced latte, try whipping instant coffee granules and water until foamy.
Then pour over cold milk for this delicious and refreshing coffee drink.

Ingredients

  • Â¼ cup boiling water
  • 1 tablespoon instant coffee granules
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 cup cold milk

Directions

  1. Step 1: Combine boiling water, coffee granules, and sugar in
    a large mug and whip with an immersion blender until foamy, about 2 minutes. Set aside.

  2. Step 2: Fill a large glass with ice cubes. Pour milk over ice
    cubes. Top with the coffee foam.
